Ideas

Capture now, connect later

A fast, autosaving long-form editor with full-text and tag search, pinning, and folders. Every idea is a card you can later drag onto the canvas and link to others.

Drag ideas around an infinite space, hover to reveal ports, pull a Bézier link between two cards, or overlap them to merge. It's a native SwiftUI scene rebuilt from scratch — no browser, no React Flow.

Under the hood

Native where it earns your trust

React Flow is a React component, so Orbit rebuilds its behavior with a native scene architecture — real SwiftUI views for sharp text, focus and accessibility, with grids, edges and ink drawn efficiently through Canvas.

  1. 1 Nodes, notes and ink are stored in world-space coordinates.
  2. 2 A shared viewport transform applies pan & zoom.
  3. 3 Nodes stay real SwiftUI views — crisp, focusable, accessible.
  4. 4 Grids, edges, previews and strokes render through SwiftUI Canvas.
  5. 5 Bézier curves attach to node borders with forgiving hit targets.
